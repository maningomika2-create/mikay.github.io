Barangay New Visayas About Us Log In Sign Up A Street-to-Home Rescue, Adoption, and Donation System

NewVisayasPetConnect is a web-based management system designed for Barangay New Visayas, Panabo City, to streamline animal rescue, pet adoption, and donation efforts. By providing a centralized digital platform, the system allows residents to report stray or lost animals while enabling administrators to manage adoption profiles and track donation allocations with full transparency. Ultimately, the platform leverages technology to enhance community safety, promote animal welfare, and ensure efficient resource management, transforming the barangay into a more organized and compassionate environment for both people and pets.

02

Flood Tracker and Relief Distribution Web System

The Flood Tracker and Relief Distribution Web System is a digital platform designed to enhance community resilience by streamlining flood reporting and the allocation of aid during emergency situations. By providing a centralized hub for real-time flood monitoring, official announcements, and transparent relief distribution management, the system replaces inefficient manual processes with a reliable interface for both residents and barangay officials. Ultimately, this tool ensures that affected families receive timely information and equitable access to resources, fostering a more organized, responsive, and accountable disaster response framework for the community.
